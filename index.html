RAMA TIRTHAM × RAMANARAYANAM

About the yatra

Hare Krishna Movement & ISKCON Visakhapatnam invite you to the Ramayana Circuit Yatra — a one-day pilgrimage to two of the region's most beloved Rama temples, Rama Tirtham and Ramanarayanam. Expect soul-stirring live kirtan, the timeless pastimes of Lord Rama narrated at the very places that breathe His glories, joyful devotional dance, and a grand lunch feast of sanctified prasadam. Bring your family and friends and come home to the Holy Name.
